Profile & Background

Alec Gayson is the Head Coach of DISS BJJ. With decades of experience across coaching, physical training and rehabilitation, Alec’s approach blends technical clarity with a strong focus on athlete wellbeing and long-term development.

As a BJJ black belt, Alec has coached service personnel and civilians, developing fundamentals through to competition-ready skillsets. Expect structured classes, detail-rich instruction and an inclusive environment.

Coaching Philosophy

“Technique first, pressure measured, progress consistent. Build solid positions, understand reactions, and develop a game you can trust under fatigue.”

Structure & clarity: sessions progress from warm-up and movement prep to focused technical work, positional study and carefully managed sparring.

Safety & longevity: emphasis on intelligent training loads, tap etiquette and protective habits so students can develop consistently.

Inclusive learning: beginners and experienced grapplers train side-by-side with adjustments for goals, experience and mobility.
