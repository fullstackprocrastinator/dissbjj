About DISS BJJ

Welcome to Diss BJJ

Diss BJJ is a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu & grappling community based in the heart of Diss, Norfolk. We provide high-quality instruction for all levels - from complete beginners to experienced competitors.

Led by BJJ black belt Alec Gayson with decades of experience as a Royal Marine Physical Training, Exercise Rehabilitation & Close Combat SME. We focus on technique, discipline, and creating a welcoming environment for everyone.

Whether you're looking to learn self-defense, get in shape, or compete at the highest levels, Diss BJJ has a program for you. Our community is built on respect, discipline, and continuous improvement.
